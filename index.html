SS26 — In Two Movements

Ready-to-wear built at the meeting point of tailoring and motion. Designed in Milan, cut in small runs.

"Tailored to move as one."

The Idea

A garment has two rhythms — the body that moves, and the cloth that holds its shape. Sync is the point where they agree.

We start from construction, not decoration. Every silhouette is drafted around a working range of motion, then pared back until only the necessary lines remain.

Small ateliers, longer lead times, fewer pieces — held to a standard that doesn't move with the season.
